Yunsheng Ma

PhD Student, Graduate Research Assistant
College of Engineering, Purdue University
Email: yunsheng [at] purdue [dot] edu

I am currently a first-year PhD student at Purdue University, Digital Twin Lab, where I serve as a graduate research assistant under the guidance of Prof. Ziran Wang. Prior to joining Purdue, I earned my Master’s degree in Computer Science from New York University. My research interests revolve around the intersection of computer vision, autonomous driving, and driver behavior modeling.
