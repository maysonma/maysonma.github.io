Yunsheng Ma

I am a PhD student at Purdue University, working in the Digital Twin Lab under the guidance of Prof. Ziran Wang. Concurrently, I am a research intern at the Bosch Center for Artificial Intelligence, where I focus on advancing foundation models for autonomous driving. I hold a Master's degree in Computer Science from New York University. My research interests include foundation models for autonomous driving, with an emphasis on vision-language models, multimodal learning, and embodied AI.
